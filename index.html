Welcome to Tamarind-Eten Preparatory School

At Tamarind-Eten Preparatory we believe teaching is touching lives forever.If children enjoy learning they will forever want to.

We recognise that every child is unique and has their own approach/ style/ understanding when learning. Our skilled and qualified teaching staff support every pupil through a wide variety of teaching and learning activities.

It's our mission to ensure that every child achieves their full potential;not only academically, but also more broadly on a social basis by exploring and excelling in diverse fields outside the classroom.
